FAQ

What is IP Planner?

IP Planner is a tool to calculate the rate that you earn Influence Points (IP) in the online game League of Legends (LOL). The rate is calculated on a player by player basis by analyzing your last 10 matches. You can then select champions you wish to unlock and IP Planner will calculate how long it will take you to get those champions.

How do I use IP Planner?

It's simple! Just enter your LOL summoner name in the Summoner Name box and select your region from the drop down. You will be shown how many Influence Points you've earned each day for your last 10 matches. Next select which champions you would like to unlock. The time it will take for you to unlock these champions will be calculated and shown at the bottom.
